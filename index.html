About Us

Welcome to Acropolis Lab, a makerspace nestled in the heart of Athens. Inspired by the rich cultural heritage of the Acropolis and powered by modern technology, we provide a collaborative environment for creators, innovators, and enthusiasts to bring their ideas to life.

Facilities & Equipment

  • Digital Fabrication: CNC Machine, Laser Cutter, 3D Printer
  • Electronics & Prototyping: Oscilloscope, Soldering Station, Computers & Controllers
  • Metalworking: Lathe, Drill Press, Grinder, Power Tools
  • Woodworking: Lathe, Woodworking Power Tools
  • General Workshop: Kiln, Hand Tools, Power Tools

Membership & Access

Acropolis Lab operates on a membership model. All memberships are handled through our Patreon page. By subscribing, you support the makerspace while gaining access to:

  • All tools and equipment (with safety training)
  • Collaboration opportunities with a diverse community
  • Workshops and tutorials on using our tools and technologies
